GET
/api/risk-score?tokens=...
Batch - up to 10 tokens
Query Parameters
tokens string required Comma-separated list of token IDs/names. Max 10. Batch responses use rule-based insights (no AI per token) for faster response times.
Example Request
GET /api/risk-score?tokens=solana,bitcoin,ethereum
Response
{
  "results": [

Pre-trade risk check
Request Body (JSON)

amount number USD value of intended trade (optional)

Risk Score Formula
Every score is calculated from three weighted signals derived from live CoinGecko market data. No black box, fully transparent and reproducible.
Volatilityweight: 40%Absolute 24h price change × 2, capped at 40. Larger swings = higher score.
Liquidityweight: 30%24h volume / market cap ratio. Thinner liquidity = harder to exit = higher score.
Momentumweight: 30%Price position within its 24h range. Near extremes = closer to reversal = higher score.
Score Levels:   Low Risk (0–33)  ·  Medium Risk (34–66)  ·  High Risk (67–100)
